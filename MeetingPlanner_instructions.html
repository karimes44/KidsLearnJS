Meeting Planner

Instructions

The Meeting Planner shows which time in California (CA) corresponds to which time in New York (NY), England (UK), Central Europe (EU), India (IN), China/Taiwan/Singapore (CN), and Japan/Korea (KR)

  1. Click the up- or down-arrows to focus on the time your are interested.
  2. The Meeting Planner assumes that people are somewhat ok to take a meeting between the hours of 7am=700 and 9pm=2100 in their respective local time.
  3. The hours cells within this time range are shown in either blue, or red.
  4. Hours cells shown in red correspond to a day later.

In the code, you can define the time zones (Zons) you are interested in and define the time off-set (Offs) between CA and the respective time zone with:

 var TIME = "Winter"
 var Zons = ["CA", "NY", "UK", "EU", "IN", "CN", "JP"];
 var Offs = [   0,    3,    8,    9,   13,   16,   17];

You can and and remove time zones as you wish, but make sure you have as many Offs as you have Zons.

The TIME variable lets you keep track if the offsets correspond to Summer Time, Winter Time, or a Transition Time, that is when for example the USA changes to Summer Time before Central Europe.
