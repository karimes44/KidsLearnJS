Monopoly Money Bank

Instructions

  1. In the fields Player 1 to Player 4 type in the name of the players and click Set Players when you are done.
  2. The Balance table shows how much money each player has in the bank in units of M=Millions.
  3. To make a transaction:
    • Select in the From field a player or the BANK.
    • In the Value field, type in the amount.
    • Also select if the amount is in units of k=Thousands or M=Millions.
    • Select in the To field a player or the BANK.
    • Click Execute Transaction
  4. You will see the transaction recorded in the Transactions table, and you will also to new bank balances in the Balance table.

... and that's all there is to it!
