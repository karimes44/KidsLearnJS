Billiards

Instructions

  • The table is set-up for a game of Nine-Ball. You can find the instructions for Nine-Ball at: Wikipedia: Nine-ball Instructions
  • To make a shot, just click where you want the cue-ball (the gray "0" one) to go.
  • You can make the cue-ball go faster or slower by adjusting the slider bar.
  • Did not like how your last shot turned out? Click Restore for another try at the same shot. The Restore button will appear as soon as all balls came to rest.
  • Should you sink the cue-ball, you can click where you want it to re-appear.
  • The program will evaluate if a shot was legal according to the rules mentioned in the first bullet point. If not the other player gets to take the next shots.
  • The program also keeps track of howmany shots each player took and which balls the player sank.
